I work as a privacy lawyer helping businesses thrive in Canada.

My goal is to help your business comply with Canadian privacy law in the least impairing way possible.

I specifically focus on crafting privacy policies, conducting privacy audits, cyber breach responses and serving as Chief Privacy Officers.

I believe Canadian privacy law is in the process of a rapid evolution, which in addition to having significantly more burdensome requirements for companies operating in Canada, will come with significantly increased enforcement mechanisms and sanctions. You can read why I believe this and what I think this means for companies operating in Canada here.
